Movie Hunt

This Movie Hunt Application was designed to help movie fanatics
search for a movie of their interest with a detailed description of the movie to help distinguish between selecting it to watch it or continuing to search for a movie.
We strongly believe our application was designed to help improve movie searching easier and give users quick feedback to make a decision based on genre, rating, and movie reviews from the New York Times.

This application was created by Alex Giannini, Alex Reveles, and Emily Chhun.

Making Movie Hunt easier for users to search for a movie at home during COVID-19.
